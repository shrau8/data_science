3. Broadcasting and vectorized operations
Broadcasting allows NumPy to perform operations on arrays of different shapes, treating smaller arrays 
as if they were the same size by "broadcasting" the smaller array.
Vectorized operations are operations that apply a function element-wise on an array without explicit loops.

integrated with the data structures from pandas.

for the same variables for a better understanding of the dataset.
